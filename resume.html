My current work covers actuarial reporting cycles, valuation result analysis, best estimate liabilities, risk adjustment, liability for remaining coverage, CSM-related analysis, Analysis of Surplus, experience investigations, capital analysis, model output review, reconciliations, quality assurance, and audit-ready reporting support.
